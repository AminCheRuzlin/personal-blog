Projects

Traditional Malay Kitchen Tools in 3D: Traditional Malay kitchen tools reflect the resourcefulness and ingenuity of the Malay community in food preparation. These tools, often crafted from natural materials like wood, stone, and coconut shells, were essential in daily cooking practices before modern kitchen appliances became widespread.

VR SportHall: FutureVR: SportHall is a virtual reality project designed to transform how people experience exhibits by immersing users in Malaysia's rich sports history. Aimed at youth aged 16 to 25, it promotes cultural heritage through interactive 3D displays, storytelling, and immersive visuals. Featuring key sports like badminton, football, athletics, and sepak takraw, the app runs on Android smartphones with VR headsets. Built in Unity, it offers an engaging, educational platform that celebrates legendary athletes and historic moments in a futuristic setting.
